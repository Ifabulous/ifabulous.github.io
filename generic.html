About Me

Hello and welcome! I am Onokhifa Uba, a dedicated Data Analyst and Artificial Intelligence enthusiast with a robust academic foundation and hands-on experience in data-driven decision-making. I am on a journey to turn data into actionable insights that propel organizational success.

Throughout my career, I have honed my skills in data collection, analysis, and visualization, working across various sectors including retail, healthcare, banking and digital marketing. My previous role as a Data Analyst, allowed me to significantly improve operational efficiencies and customer engagement through meticulous data analysis and strategic insights.

My technical proficiency spans a broad array of tools including Python, SQL, Power BI, and Tableau, and Excel which I leverage to create transparent, user-friendly dashboards and reports that facilitate informed decision-making and strategic planning. My commitment to continuous learning and improvement is paralleled by my dedication to integrity and hard work, values that I bring to every project and team.

I believe in the power of data to not only support but also transform businesses. As a friendly and collaborative team player, I am always eager to join forces with other professionals to tackle challenges and uncover opportunities within piles of data.

I am passionate about automating business processes and leveraging the latest innovations in machine learning and predictive analytics to enhance business outcomes. My goal is to work with forward-thinking organizations where I can contribute to exciting projects and help navigate the complexities of the digital age.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I look forward to connecting with like-minded professionals and exploring how we can drive future success together.
